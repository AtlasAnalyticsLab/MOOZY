# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions.
- Give and accept feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the community and the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Unacceptable behavior can be reported to yousefkotp@outlook.com. Reports will be reviewed and addressed promptly.

This project follows the spirit of the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
